Master Chief

The Master Chief is a Spartan-II who went and faught some crazy aliens know as the "Covenant". They were pretty rad and dangerous, so the Master Chief came in and faught against them till they were defeated. With the help of him and the UNSC, which is the space navy, they won the war and humanity was safe.

Games

The Halo games stem back all the way from Halo CE to Halo Infinite currently. The first games were made by Bungie until Halo CE: Anniversery where 343 took over the mantle and still are the ones to this day.

Weapons

One of the artifacts that Halo is known for is their collection of ICONIC weapons. Halo has weapons such as the assault rifle , needler, plasma pistol, magnum, spnker rocket luncher, and much more in which this franchise has accumulated.
